Hey,

I don’t know if you expected this,
but I wanted to say one thing — calmly.

Sometimes people don’t get the chance
to say things properly,
and I didn’t want that to be my case.

What I felt was genuine.
No pressure. No expectations.
Just something honest that mattered to me.

I understand silence.
I respect distance.
And I respect whatever this moment means to you.

You don’t owe me a reply.
I just didn’t want my last words
to be left unsaid.

— Keshav
